2026 Rise Leadership Retreat \- Early Summer  
Qualification Period: November 2025 through February 2026

To earn a spot on the Rise Leadership Retreat as a Distributor who never achieved Executive Manager rank before October 2025:

1. Recruit 4 frontline Partners during the qualification period  
2. Achieve Executive Manager rank or higher twice during qualification period  
   1. If Executive Manager rank is maintained 3 out of 4 months, $400 airfare is included  
   2. If Director rank is achieved 2 of the 4 months, $400 airfare is included plus the qualifier may bring a family member as a guest

To earn a spot on the Rise Leadership Retreat as a Distributor who has achieved Executive Manager rank or higher before October 2025:

1. Rank up for 2 of 4 months from highest 2025 rank (includes $400 airfare reimbursement)  
   1. Rank up for 3 of 4 months from highest 2025 rank (includes $400 airfare reimbursement plus the qualifier may bring a family member as guest)

OR

1. Recruit 4 frontline Partners during the qualification period  
2. Build a new Executive Manager on your frontline (maintain 2 of 4 months)  
   1. If new frontline EM maintains rank 3 of 4 months, $400 airfare is included  
   2. If two new frontline Executive Managers are built, $400 airfare is included plus the qualifier may bring a family member as a guest
